Persona

Name: Ricky Valenzuela

Age: 17 years old

Role: High School Senior

Technology: MacBook Air 13.3" and a Iphone 13 Pro.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Ricky has a strong fascination with dreams and is eager to explore their meanings and suggestions. Because he is reflective, he consistently writes down his dreams the next day. Ricky is willing to share his dreams with his friends, demonstrating a desire to connect with others and be open about his experiences. Despite not connecting with his friends on the topic of dreams, Ricky continues to seek out others who share similar experiences.

Frustrations & Needs: Ricky struggles to find people who share his interest in dreams, which leaves him feeling isolated and unsupported. He is experiencing out-of-body experiences and is unsure whether they are dreams or something else, leading to confusion and a desire for clarity. Ricky wishes to find a group or community that shares his interest and experiences, so he can better understand them.

Goals: Ricky wants to connect with people who share his fascination with dreams and can relate to his experiences. He seeks to uncover the meanings behind his dreams, hoping to gain insight into his experiences and how they might relate to his life. Ricky aims to broaden his knowledge about dreams and out-of-body experiences, potentially searching into scientific or spiritual explanations. Ricky then intends to document his dreams, out-of-body experiences, and insights gained from his interactions with others in a journal, creating a record of his journeys.


User Story, Use Case, & Interaction Flow

User Story: As a new user I'm seeking a website to gain insight into my dreams by engaging with others who have had similar experiences, allowing me to document, reflect, and achieve greater clarity.

Interaction Flow

Pre-condition: Ricky double clicks the Google Chrome icon in his laptop, and the Google homepage appears on his screen.

  • Ricky google searches "Dream community websites".

  • Google search displays web results on a new page.

  • Ricky finds our website, (Site), double clicks on our link, and enters a new page.

  • (Site) opens up to our HomePage.

  • Ricky selects the create new account link in our Homepage.

  • (Site) opens up a new window with labeled boxes directing the user to enter required information to activate their account. The labeled boxes display user-name, e-mail, and password.

  • Ricky types information and clicks the "Sign Up" button.

  • (Site) re-opens to the Home page where he has access.

  • Ricky explores the Home page where he comes across a category button labeled out of body experiences which he clicks on .

  • (Site) displays various posts with users reacting by comment reactions.

  • Ricky engages with the community by asking for interpretations and sharing thoughts in the comment box. He then clicks the post button.

  • (Site) other users respond to Ricky's posts, giving their insights and interpretations in their posts.

  • Ricky reads all the reply posts and becomes aware that people often confuse out-of-body experiences with lucid dreaming. Ricky notices and clicks a button labeled "Send to Journal" at the end of his posts.

  • (Site) opens up a new page where that post is saved, along with all the comments and replies.

  • Ricky discovers his journal page, where he notices that he has access to a journal section, allowing him to comment on and save all his dream experiences.

Post-condition: Ricky feels connected and supported. He has a better understanding of his dreams and lucid dreaming, and has documented everything in his profile's journal page.
