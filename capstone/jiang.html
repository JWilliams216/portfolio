Persona

Name

Jiang Mao

Profession

Jiang works from home for Google, but in her free time is a professional moderator. She runs her own discord channel, mods for a few of her favorite Twitch streamers, and for a subreddit.

Technology

Macbook Pro and iMac both running macOS Monterey. iPhone 13 Pro Max with iOS 15. iPad Pro with iPadOS 15

Attitudes and Behaviors

Jiang is an authoritarian, favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority. She believes that everything runs best when everyone follows the rules, especially for a website. That means no posting on tags that aren't relevant and no memes in #general. She is an ENTJ, the Commander. Very decisive and well organized, Jiang takes pride in her great leadership skills and feels she knows what's best for the community's she is apart of.

Frustrations and Needs

  • When there is no structure, order, or rules to abide by
  • Spam/promoters, abuse/harassment, anything that causes a negative experience to users on a social media site.
  • Needs to be able to have features such as ban user, delete post, and being able to approve post submissions.

Goal(s)

Her main goals when moderating is to make all user's have an easy and positive experience. Bringing down the (hammer) of justice to all spammers and toxic users. She also dedicates her time to mediate any disputes between users.

User Story

The admin user wants a ban function to achieve the goal of cleaning up the website of irrelevant posts. Enabling admin users to do this would deliver a more positive community and environment promoting dream posters to want to share more.

Interaction Flow

Jiang finishes up a long day of work and decides to log onto her dream account to review some of the most recent post submissions made that day to lighten her mood. Right after logging in she sees that one user in particular keeps submitting hateful spam messages to purposefully bog the server and prevent others posts from being seen. She makes an executive decision that this person shouldn't have access with their account anymore for posting and permanently bans that user. Bye-bye!

Pre-condition

Jiang is logged into her admin account.

Post-condition

A toxic user is banned from using their account to post on the dream board.

Flow Steps

  • Jiang opens the dream site on her browser.
  • She navigates to the log-in section
  • The site pulls up a login box, and she enters her admin credentials
  • The site immediate directs her to most recent posts
  • She clicks on the user she wishes to ban
  • Since she is in her admin account the website offers her features such as ban
  • She clicks on permanently ban user
  • The site the gives a list of reasons why they are getting banned
  • She selects Hateful Speech and clicks done
