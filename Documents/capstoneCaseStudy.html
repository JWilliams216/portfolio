Dreamery Case Study

Executive Summary

Dreamery is designed to be a platform for people to anonymously post their dreams and share their experiences with others, it has a high-level vision to provide a supportive and inclusive community where users can interact with each other in a positive manner.

To maintain a positive and safe environment, Dreamery has a terms of use that prohibits bullying, explicit content, threats, and the sharing of personal information.

The goal is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly experience for users to share their dreams, connect with others, and keep track of their own dream journey in a personal and meaningful way.

Conclusion

Testing & Quality Assurance: Insomnia was used to be able to test functions and help find bugs.

Results & Impact: Dreamery has the potential to be a fully functioning app that is a space for individuals to post their dreams and connect with others regarding their dreams. Inn the future I want to implement some of the functions the timeframe didn't allow us to implement.

Lessons Learned: Code freeze is extremely important and no last minute fixes, additions, or merges should be attempted because it could break something else.

Conclusion: Working as a team to create an app or website while learning the skills needed to complete the project has given me the opportunity to work on my coping mechanisms. First I will try to ignore the frustration and continue working, then take a step back and reflect on how to resolve the issue, then come back and address it with whomever necessary, and continue the work.
