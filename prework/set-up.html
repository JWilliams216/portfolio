Purpose, Audience, & Goals

Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio website.

Audience: The website is tailored towards professionals in the web development and I.T. community nationwide, with a specific focus on startup founders, hiring managers, recruiters, and peers.

Goal: The primary goal of this site is to enable users to reach out to me for potential employment and collaboration opportunities via the contact form or my linked social media profiles. Additionally, the website serves as a platform to exhibit my work and cultivate a refined professional online presence, which is its secondary purpose..

Persona

Name: Natasha Willard

Role: Recruiter for tech jobs.

Technology: iMac 24" with Studio Display, MacBook Air with M2 chip, iPad Pro 12.9", Apple Watch Ultra, and Apple TV 4K.

Behaviors & Attitude: Works 8am to 5pm trying to connect jobs with individuals who are qualified. Spends countless hours on LinkedIn connecting with different companies and works with local colleges and esteemed bootcamps for potential employees.

Situation or Scenario: A job has landed in her lap, Natasha did a LinkedIn search for a few keywords and came across a profile that has this site in their profile.

Frustrations: Natasha is having a tough time finding a junior web developer with the skills to fill the position.

Goal(s): Wants to see if profile has the skills and qualifications for job.
