Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections:

Navigation bar: Sticky and interactive to different sections of site.

Welcome: An introduction about me that states my name on top of an image.

About Me: This section will detail my skill set, showcase my strengths, and personality. Specific skills (CSS, HTML, Bootstrap 5.2, etc.).

Portfolio: Work I've completed within the bootcamp, will be sure to update as I create more.

Contact: This section will contain a contact form (name, email, subject, message, submit and reset buttons below), my professional email address, LinkedIn, and GitHub.

Footer: Images of GitHub, email, and LinkedIn with links attached to the images.

Design: Simple, color where important, very clear, spaced out, not cluttered or overwhelming to look at.
